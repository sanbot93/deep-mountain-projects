Commons · Proof of authorship

Serviceberry

A gift-economy network for creators. Upload a work, sign it, and receive a tamper-proof certificate of authorship — hashed, watermarked, and witnessed by a community-run chain of Raspberry Pis and LoRaWAN gateways. No fees, no wallets, no extraction.

About this workshop

Deep Mountain Sound is Santi's home for music and the tools around it. The threads run together: three of these projects live inside Ableton Live, one organises the life that surrounds the music, and one protects the work once it leaves the studio. Built part-time, in the open, from Nelson, BC.

“Move with what matters.”
